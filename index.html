Terms and Conditions

This survey will take approximately 15 minutes. You are asked to answer some questions about seismic engineering and virtual presentations. You will be asked the same questionnaire before and after watching a video presentation. Please be honest with your answers.

Data Collection

Your data will be stored and processed anonymously only for the purpose of this study. We do guarantee privacy is completely preserved. However, we do not guarantee any data access by third-party applications. Please refer to the policies of the third-party applications (Google Forms). In accordance with good scientific conduct and aiming to support open science and research practice, the data set may be published online for open and free data access together with any accompanying research report in a fully anonymized manner. This allows other researchers to build on our work.

Risk And Benefits

There are no risks associated with your participation. We can not offer you any direct benefits from participating in this study; however, you will support scientific research.

Participant's Rights

If you have read this informed consent and have decided to participate in the survey, please understand your participation is voluntary and you have the right to withdraw your consent or discontinue participation at any time without penalty or loss of benefits to which you are otherwise entitled. If you withdraw from the survey while participating or within 14 days after your participation, all existing data which we collect from your participation will be deleted. If you withdraw more than 14 days after your participation, we will still attempt to remove all data collected from you but this may not be possible due to the analysis process. The results of this study may be presented in scientific articles.
